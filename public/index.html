About the website...

Currently our country, India is going through an unprecedented period of uncertainty, people are losing their livelihood for which they have travelled thousands of kms in some cases. As the migrant workers are returning home and losing their daily wage, we try to help them by providing an opportunity to work and earn near their home. This is not just a massive chance for the workers but also the regional industries who have lost their majority force due to the Coronavirus Crisis.

The website in general is easy to use with login/signup pages provided on the top right corner of the page. Register and find the job listings provided by various employers.
